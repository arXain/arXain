About Us

Why arχain?

arχain is an Ethereum Dapp that deals with all the aspects of a peer-reviewed journal service. Peer-review is critical to modern science, engineering, and healthcare endeavors. However, the system for implementing this process has lagged behind and results in expensive costs for publishing and accessing material, long turn around times reminiscent of snail-mail, and shockingly opaque editorial practices.


The recent advance of Blockchain and Smart Contract technology (Ethereum) coupled with decentralized file sharing networks (IPFS) naturally lead us to believe that traditional journal and editorial structure could evolve. We created our manuscript distribution and reviewing platform based on the arXiv, but in a completely decentralized manner. Users utilize, maintain, and grow the network of scholarship simply by interacting with the arχain.

The Team

The first interation of the arχain was created at PennAppsXVII by 4 physics PhD candidates from the University of Pennsylvania. They continue to maintain and develop the platform to make it the best and most efficient journal it can be.
